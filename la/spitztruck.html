Our first SpitzTruck was opened by Enrique Cajero and Manuel Villegas in Los Angeles in May, 2017. Prior to taking ownership of his their first Spitz, both Enrique and Manuel worked in several LA Spitz locations as sandwich artisans and managers. The pair are joined by a handful of longtime Spitz family members and a great new crew!

We're so excited about our new truck, especially with Enrique and Manny at the helm. We hope to see you soon!
